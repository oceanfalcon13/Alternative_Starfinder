Building a ship: Things to consider

Ships are built using a frame and components, each costing credits. Civilian ships, though called different things, use the military size chart for crew, ac, health, etc. They are locked to civilian grade shields and armor. Shields cost (1k * 1.5(tier) * (10 * size)). Armor costs (1.5k * 1.5(tier) * (15 * size)).
